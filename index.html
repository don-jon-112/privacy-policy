Privacy Policy

Twitch Extension
Extension: Fishing Game Last updated: May 2025 Effective: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Fishing Game Twitch Extension ("Extension", "we", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you interact with it on Twitch. By using the Extension, you agree to the practices described here.

1 Information we collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to run the game:

Twitch User ID

Your unique Twitch user ID, provided by the Twitch Extension framework via the Identity Link (ID Share) feature. This is a numeric identifier — not your username or email address.

Twitch Display Name / Login

When you grant identity access, we may also receive your Twitch login name to display inside the game and to link your account across sessions.

Game Progress Data

Fish caught, coin balance, inventory items (rods, boats, bait, lures), equipped gear, and fishing history — all generated by your in-game activity.

We do not collect your email address, real name, payment information, IP address, or any sensitive personal information.

2 How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Identifying your account and loading your saved game progress
  • Storing fish catches, coins, and inventory between sessions
  • Displaying your stats within the Extension panel
  • Linking your Twitch ID to your game account across visits
  • Improving Extension stability and fixing bugs

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the game.

3 Twitch Identity Link (ID Share)

This Extension uses Twitch's Identity Link feature. When you click "Connect Twitch Account", Twitch shares your opaque user ID (and optionally your login name) with the Extension so we can create and load your account. This is an explicit, opt-in action — the Extension will not receive your identity until you grant permission.

You can revoke this permission at any time through your Twitch connection settings .

4 Data storage & security

Your game data is stored in Google Firebase / Firestore, a secure cloud database. Data is transmitted over HTTPS and protected by Firebase's industry-standard security rules.

We do not share your data with third parties, sell it, or use it outside of the Extension's functionality.

5 Data retention

Your game data is retained for as long as you actively use the Extension. If you request deletion, we will permanently remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

6 Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request permanent deletion of your account and game data
  • Correction — request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Withdrawal — revoke ID Share consent at any time via Twitch settings

7 Children's privacy

This Extension is intended for general audiences on Twitch and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Twitch's own Terms of Service require users to be at least 13 years old.

8 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the Extension or applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
